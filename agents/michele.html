Biography

One of P.U.S.'s newest investigators and an avid gamer who transfers her sharp reflexes directly into real-world combat. Michele joined P.U.S. to protect Dream Town alongside seniors Kokona and Nobunaga. She wears a cat-ear headset and has a mechanical tail, reflecting her love of cats. Energetic, cheerful, and deeply loyal to her faction, she's a natural recon agent who never misses what's hiding around a corner.

Abilities

Passive Kitty Tracer

Enemies that hit Michele with direct fire are revealed through walls for 2 seconds, allowing her team to track their position.

Active Pawtector

Michele deploys a Pawtector device that sticks to surfaces, automatically attacking and slowing nearby enemies within its range.

Ultimate Mighty Meowblast

Michele deploys a powerful automated turret that attacks all nearby enemies within a large range, dealing massive damage.
