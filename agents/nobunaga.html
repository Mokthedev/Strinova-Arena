Biography

Nobunaga is the Captain of the First Investigation Squad of P.U.S. Though he may appear cold and ruthless, he is actually incredibly kind and straightforward. After being betrayed by his dear brother, Nobunaga leaves no stone unturned in pursuit of the truth. His most powerful weapons are Guardian Eye, the pulse radar he carries with him at all times, and his unwavering will.

Abilities

Passive Blinding Shot

Charged shots in ADS mode cause enemies to lose weapon accuracy.

Active Guardian Eye

Deploy a Guardian Eye to boost Rate of Fire, Weapon Usability, and regenerate Armor when in range. Connect Guardian Eyes to enhance these bonuses.

Ultimate Pulse Overload

Deploy a Pulse Device that periodically emits waves, revealing and silencing hit enemies.
