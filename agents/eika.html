Biography

A high school student from Preton who aspires to join The Scissors. Eika has a deep fondness for her hometown and deeply distrusts P.U.S., which abandoned it during a disaster known as the Sunset. Her parents operate a family restaurant where she helps out as a delivery worker. In her free time she practices guitar and plays in a band with her friends. Using her String Energy to control flames, she transforms into a masked, mysterious hero silently guarding the streets of Preton from the shadows.

Abilities

Passive Heat Buildup

Each shot on an enemy accumulates Heat. At Max Heat, DMG against stringified enemies increases significantly. Skills automatically consume Heat for enhanced effects.

Active Flame Cage

Summon a Flame Cage at target location. While you and enemies are inside the cage, your Primary Weapon inflicts extra Burn DMG. Enemies crossing the cage walls take Burn DMG, with additional bonus DMG when at full Heat.

Ultimate Fireball

Throw an explosive fireball that deals extra DMG to stringified enemies. Consuming 100 Heat grants a significant DMG boost and applies a slowing effect to all enemies hit.
