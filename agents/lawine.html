Biography

The tutorial character of Strinova and the stealth specialist of The Scissors. Lawine betrayed P.U.S. to join The Scissors, believing in their cause to return humanity to Earth. She excels at creating her own hunting grounds where she can dip in and out of combat with impunity. Between stealth uses she functions as a midlaner who marks enemy positions and applies constant offensive pressure, making her one of the most versatile agents in the game.

Abilities

Passive Scan Pulse

Lawine's active skill sends out scan pulses that reveal nearby enemies to the entire team for a short duration.

Active Shadow Scan

Lawine throws a scan dagger that immediately reveals all enemies within range on impact. Can be upgraded to also slow revealed enemies.

Ultimate Stealth Zone

Creates a Stealth Zone where Lawine becomes invisible. She can now fire, use skills, and throw grenades while in stealth, but must wait 5 seconds before re-entering stealth after doing so.
