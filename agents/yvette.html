Biography

Yvette is a genius P.U.S researcher who carries a teddy bear named “Fay” with her wherever she goes. Though introverted, she deeply trusts her friends. In combat, she can lock down the battlefield by summoning “Fay” to suppress the enemy.

Abilities

Passive Camouflage

Stand still to enter camo stealth, becoming visible only as enemies get closer.

Active Teddy Assault

Summon Fay to charge at enemies, leaving Ice on collision, causing them to slip and become more vulnerable.

Ultimate Frostfall

Summon a Frost Bear to create Polar Zones, causing enemies to Slip, become Vulnerable, and lowering their Rate of Fire and Weapon Usability.
