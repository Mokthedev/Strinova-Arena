Biography

An artist, magician, and art dealer of Urbino, Galatea is the older and more celebrated sister of Maddelena. She travels Strinova with theatrical flair, creating elaborate illusions mid-combat. Her teleportation-based kit makes her the most mobile agent in the game and the hardest to master. Her passive leaves decoys every time she unsticks from a wall. Her ultimate deploys multiple teleportation decoys simultaneously, making her position completely unknowable to enemies.

Abilities

Passive Deception

Galatea leaves behind a decoy whenever she unsticks from a wall. Decoys detect nearby enemies and briefly expose those who attack them. Passive only goes on cooldown if a decoy is damaged.

Active Wild Card

Galatea throws a card in an arc. On impact with ground or wall, it creates a decoy she can teleport to. Enemies attacking the decoy are exposed and briefly blinded.

Ultimate Mirage

Galatea tosses multiple cards forward, each creating a teleportation decoy in different positions simultaneously, making her location completely unpredictable.
