Biography

Once a senior member of P.U.S., Celestia broke away after the Collapse Syndrome devastated Strinova and founded Astral Sanctuary — a healing organization outside any faction's control. She joined Urbino as a centrist who believes in resolution over conflict. Her entire kit is built around keeping her team's armor at maximum efficiency. She's the purest support in the game, and the backbone of any Urbino team composition.

Abilities

Passive Cosmic Recovery

Celestia gradually restores her own armor and that of all nearby allies at 2 per second, providing constant passive sustain throughout engagements.

Active Starlight

Celestia grants an ally a temporary armor boost and begins charging energy. When fully charged, she can teleport to their location.

Ultimate Astral Gate

Celestia grants massive armor to a chosen ally and begins charging. When fully charged she teleports to their location. Pressing ultimate again cancels the teleport but keeps the armor boost.
